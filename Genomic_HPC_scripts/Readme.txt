Here in this folder is all the bash scripts that were used on the UPPMAX to analysis this data with the relevant slurm commands.

Remember to download the raw data from the European Nucleotide Archive Project: PRJEB38313 (https://www.ebi.ac.uk/ena/browser/view/PRJEB38313?show=reads).

Also remember to have all the listed dependencies below.
-FastQC (Wingett and Andrews, 2018)
-MultiQC (Ewels et al., 2016)
-Trim Galore (Babraham Bioinformatics, 2020)
-Unicycler (Wick et al., 2017)
-QUAST (Gurevich et al., 2013)
-KmerFinder (Larsen et al., 2014)
-ARIBA (Hunt et al., 2017)
-ResFinder (Zankari et al., 2012)
-PointFinder (Zankari et al., 2017)
-Prokka (Seemann, 2014)
-Roary (Page et al., 2015)
-IQ-Tree2 (Quang et al., 2020)

Remember to change X in the bash scripts to your needs, best of luck.
